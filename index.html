PROBLEM

Crypto ecosystems are stuck in endless cycle of pumps and dumps, high yield stakings that often inflate the token supply leaving early investors rekt. The larger population in core ecosystem is made up of crypto newbies who, more often than not, FOMO in these kinds of tokens and get stuck forever. Over the years, this problem scares everyday people from exploring myriad opportunities. Hence, slows adoption.

SOLUTION

SatoshiCash implements an outstanding ‘deflationary’ model and ‘secure’ token ethics. The smart contract is open sourced and verified for averyone to conduct their own research Liquidity and team tokens locked. Thus, we have achieved a fair crypto token which increases the value of each STC token by every transaction made. A 4% transaction fee is deducted from every transaction as follows: 3% burnt forever, 1% sent to a marketing wallet for marketing purposes. With core chain’s extreme low fees, secure and scalable blockchain technology, SatoshiCash brings DeFi at the finger-tips of everyday people with endless opportunities.
