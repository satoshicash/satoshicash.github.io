SOLUTION

SatoshiCash implements an outstanding ‘deflationary’ model and ‘secure’ token ethics. The smart contract is open sourced and verified for averyone to conduct their own research Liquidity and team tokens locked. Thus, we have achieved a fair crypto token which increases the value of each STC token by every transaction made. With core chain’s extreme low fees, secure and scalable blockchain technology, SatoshiCash brings DeFi at the finger-tips of everyday people with endless opportunities.
